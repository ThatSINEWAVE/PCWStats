Privacy Policy

Welcome to PCWStats! This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users of our site. PCWStats does not use cookies, trackers, or any other methods to collect information about your browser type and version, operating system, referring site, or the date and time of access.

2. Use of Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information to use or analyze. Your privacy is respected and maintained by the design of our site.

3. Cookies and Trackers

PCWStats does not use any cookies or tracking technologies. We do not track your activities or collect any information about your usage of the site.

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Security

We do not collect personal data; hence, data security measures are not applicable for personal information. Our focus is on maintaining a secure website experience.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
