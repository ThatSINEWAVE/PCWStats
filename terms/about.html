Who Created This Site?

PCWStats was created by ThatSINEWAVE on GitHub, also known as SINEWAVE within the Project CW community. The creation of the site was possible with the help of community members who providing feedback and support while making the site.

The site was developed with the purpose of providing players of Project CW with a simple yet powerful tool to view and compare tank statistics, analyze maps, and create builds for agents within the game.
